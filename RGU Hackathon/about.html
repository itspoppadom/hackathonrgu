There's more to it than Lifts and Toilets

Our initial idea has been to create a basic interactive view of Level 3 at the Sir Ian Wood Building, We know that this building has many things to offer across 6 floors, and with a map redesign that we decided to go for the Building Plans we'd require a lot more time to achieve that.

Cafeteria

On level 3, you have the option to take the stairs down a level to the extensive cafeteria which is equipped in Costa, Subway, 53.3 Degrees, FreshRGU, and Fresh off the Chopping board.
